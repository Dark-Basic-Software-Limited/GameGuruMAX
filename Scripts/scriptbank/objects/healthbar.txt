Simple Healthbar

Will display viewed enemy health in a bar or text, set Always Active.
Set the DISPLAY RANGE
Set the DISPLAY MODE for 'Bar' or 'Text'

To use add place the Health Bar object somewhere on your map 
it will be hidden on startup. It should be automatically linked to 
the healthbar behaviour, if not then link the behavior to the 
Health Bar object. Thats it. Now a targeted enemy with in 
range will display their health bar shown above and 
will turn red when their health is below 80.
