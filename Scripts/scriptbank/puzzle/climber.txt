Climber 
Will create a climable area where placed

Select the climber object and place on the map where you wish a climbing area.
Adjust its height and size to suit the. The climber will be invisbile in game. 
Check the editor marker box to see it in game (just for testing purposes).
Make adjustments in the behaviour to suit. 
You should now be able to climb the designated area in game.
If you have problems gripping the climber either adjust its size, shape
or adjust the Grip adjustment option.
