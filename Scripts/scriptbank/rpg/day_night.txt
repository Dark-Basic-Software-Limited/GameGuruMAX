DayNight Behavior
This object will be treated as a Day/Night Time Cycler. Set ALLWAYS ON

Behavior Settings
START_ANGLE=-95(-95,95)		The suns starting angle
TIME_DILATION=1(1,10)		The time cycle dilation (speed adjustment)
DIAGNOSTICS!=0			Checkbix for diagnostic readout
MIN_AMBIENCE_R=10(0,255)	Minimum R Ambience Setting at Night
MIN_AMBIENCE_G=25(0,255)	Minimum G Ambience Setting at Night
MIN_AMBIENCE_B=75(0,255)	Minimum B Ambience Setting at Night
MIN_EXPOSURE#=0.45(0.01,1.00) Minimum Exposure Setting at Night
MAX_AMBIENCE_R=255(0,255)	Maximum R Ambience Setting in Day
MAX_AMBIENCE_G=255(0,255)	Maximum G Ambience Setting in Day
MAX_AMBIENCE_B=255(0,255)	Maximum B Ambience Setting in Day
MAX_EXPOSURE#=1.00(0.01,1.00) Maximum Exposure Setting in Day

Will trigger logic linked objects on nightfall
